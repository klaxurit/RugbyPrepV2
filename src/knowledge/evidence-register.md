# Evidence Register — RugbyPrep Hard Thresholds

> This document records all numerical thresholds ("hard values") used in the app.
> Each entry specifies the source, evidence level, and last verification date.
> Purpose: ensure clinical defensibility with professional strength & conditioning coaches.

## Evidence Levels
- **A**: Systematic review / meta-analysis
- **B**: RCT or high-quality prospective cohort (rugby/team sport specific)
- **C**: Expert consensus, observational study, or single high-quality cohort
- **D**: Expert opinion, practitioner recommendation, physiological rationale

## Notes on Evidence Quality

- Most rugby-specific data (CMJ, sprint) comes from elite international/professional players. Amateur baselines may differ by 15–30%.
- ACWR literature is contested (Windt & Gabbett 2019 debate). The 0.8–1.3 sweet spot is widely adopted in team sports practice despite methodological limitations.
- 1RM formulas are validated up to 10 reps; beyond that, estimation error increases significantly.
- All thresholds should be reviewed annually as evidence evolves.
