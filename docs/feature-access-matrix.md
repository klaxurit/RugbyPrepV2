# RugbyForge — Free vs Premium

## Product Rules

- Safety is never premium-gated.
- Core training access remains available in `free`.
- Premium unlocks depth, automation, and advanced interpretation.

## Free

- `program_basic`
  - Week program generation
  - ACWR-based safety adjustments
  - Rehab routing and prehab
- `notifications_basic`
  - Standard training reminders
- `calendar_basic`
  - Club schedule and match calendar
- `athletic_tests_basic`
  - Test entry and basic result tracking

## Premium

- `premium_program_adaptations`
  - Auto-generated next-target suggestions
  - More contextual progression guidance
- `premium_analytics`
  - Detailed charts
  - Baseline comparisons by position/level
  - Regression flags and season trend reading
- `advanced_notifications`
  - Reserved for custom reminder logic and richer templates
- `coach_mode`
  - Reserved for future coach/team workflows
- `priority_support`
  - Reserved for support routing and SLA

## Explicit Non-Gated Safety Features

- ACWR warnings and deload protection
- Injury exclusions and contraindication filtering
- Rehab phases and safety fallbacks
- Base prehab content
